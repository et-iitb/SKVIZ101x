Fundamentals of 3D Visualization

Preface

Architects, designers, photographers, physicists, astronomers, fashion designers, filmmakers, numerical control programmers, and surgeons are some of the satisfying careers in today's world. But, what do they have in common? Achieving success in all of these requires excellent 3D visualization skills!

One challenge faced by aspirants is lack of guidelines to develop their visualization skills as they move from concepts to application. In this course, participants will become familiar with the different aspects of 3D visualization, particularly spatial intelligence and its application.

How To Use This Book

All the topics can be accessed from the Index page, which is accessible from the link in the lower section. To navigate to the next or previous pages, use the Next or Back arrows. Within each chapter are different Learning Dialogues (LeDs) or recorded lectures. Following each dialogue are practice questions. You can attempt the questions multiple times until you are clear with the concept.
